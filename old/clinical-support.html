Pharmacy

Our pharmacists are an important link between our doctors and patients. We aim to provide safe, timely and cost-effective healthcare and equip our patients and their caregivers with the required knowledge to best manage their medical conditions.

Outpatient Pharmacy

  • Medication counselling and other specialised counselling

  • Generic drug substitution

  • Medication reconciliation

  • Inpatient Pharmacy

Our pharmacists support our medical teams by

  • Reviewing the medication chart and advising on the cost and dosage of drugs prescribed, and drug interactions

  • Medication counselling

  • Medication reconciliation

  • Providing therapeutic drug monitoring

Retail Pharmacy

  • Minor ailments consultation and advice on proper self-medication

  • Over-the-counter medicines, vitamins and mineral supplements, food supplements, diabetic care, sports injury care (soon to start) and specialised skincare (soon to start)

Inhouse Laboratory

24 x 7 in-house laboratory to perform all blood tests. You need not to rush in emergency hours. Our expertise are round-the-clock available.
